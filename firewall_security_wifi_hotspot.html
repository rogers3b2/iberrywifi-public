Highlights of Our Firewall and Wi-Fi Hotspot Services

Category-Based Filtering is next genration firewall and essential for various environments. In Schools and Educational Institutions, it allows the blocking of inappropriate content, such as adult websites, social media, and gaming sites, while whitelisting educational resources to promote focused learning. This approach ensures compliance with regulations like the CIPA (Children’s Internet Protection Act). For Businesses and Corporates, it restricts access to non-work-related sites, including entertainment, shopping, and gambling, while permitting access to essential business tools. Implementing a network firewall can enhance security in these settings. In Public Spaces, it blocks malicious or harmful content to protect users and allows for customized filters based on the audience, such as family-friendly settings in malls.

Intrusion Detection and Prevention System (IDPS) plays a crucial role in network security. It offers Real-Time Threat Detection to identify and block malicious activities, including DDoS attacks, malware, and unauthorized access attempts. The system features a hardware firewall that provides robust protection against threats. With Comprehensive Monitoring, it continuously observes network traffic for anomalies and potential breaches. Managed firewall services can further enhance security by providing expert oversight. Custom Alerts provide instant notifications for critical security events, ensuring effective firewall protection.

Seamless Wi-Fi Access enhances user experience by providing hassle-free internet connectivity through multiple authentication options, such as social logins (Facebook, Google), SMS-based OTP (One-Time Password), and voucher codes for time-limited access. A web-based firewall can be integrated to secure these connections. It also supports captive portals that redirect users to a splash page upon connection.
